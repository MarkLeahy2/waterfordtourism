Outside Waterford

Greenway

The Greenway may start at the edge of the city but it’s a lovely 40km cycle up to Dungarvan with a lot of beautiful sights along the way.

Dunmore

Dunmore is about a 20-minute drive from the city. It has many lovely beaches but what stands out is a most enjoyable cliff walk.

Tramore

Tramore is about a 15-minute drive from the city. Tramore offers probably the best beach in Waterford stretching into the distance offering a brilliant walk.

Comeragh Mountains

The Comeragh Mountains are about a 30-minute drive from the city. There are many walks to choose from depending on what part you go to, probably the best views you will get Waterford

Mount Congreve

Mount Congreve is about a 5-minute drive outside the city. Originally being a privately owned then gifted to the government acts as an attraction to many offering the most wonderful and diverse garden Waterford can offer.

Copper Coast Drive

The Copper Coast drive is a stretch of road along the coast about 20 minutes from the city. It offers a very scenic drive with many coves along the way.
