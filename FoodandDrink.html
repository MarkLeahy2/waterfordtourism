Food and Drink

For more information about availability and opening times of the these restuarants listed, click on the titles below

Bell Pepper

If you are looking for something to eat thats a little more diverse in the city. Bell Pepper is the place to go! Bell Pepper opened its doors in 2017, offering healthy, authentic Thai street food cooked and served in a fast-casual setup. Not only that but their food has been clinically proven to boast your health.

Shush

Shush Waterford is located on The Mall in Waterford City in the Heart of Waterford’s Viking Triangle. Shush, a first-floor evening bar, is set in plush décor of dark timbers, rich red furnishings, and leather. Shush provides the perfect setting for all occasions from a Thursday evening post work cocktail to a special birthday celebration.

Peppers Restaurant

​Peppers Restaurant's pedigree, enviable location and stunning interior design makes it one of Waterford Cities most finest Indian cuisine. Peppers is undoubtedly one of Southern Ireland’s Best Indian Restaurants. All food is sourced locally and all Spice is grounded directly from their kitchen.

Pinks

Pinks Cantina & Grill is dedicated to providing high quality food service and excellent customer service. Pinks will offer an authentic Mexican menu in a fun, energetic, colourful and exciting environment. Pinks will offer fun & casual drinking, small plates or larger sharing plates, lunch, happy hour and dinner.

Berkana

Gourmet Italian bakery and cafe serving home-made pasta, focaccia, salads, quiches and authentic italian specialities as well as artisan gelato (ice cream) and of course the best coffee in town! Berkana represents blossoming into a new start in life. To us, it represents the authentic, healthy goodness of their food.

Uluru

If you are in to some cusine "Down-Under", the Uluru has a well-deserved reputation for casual dining, serving traditional homemade dishes. As the Aussie would say ‘enjoy a bit of Tucker’ from their famed Darwin sizzlers. .Their extensive menu caters for all appetites ranging from light bites to full courses.
